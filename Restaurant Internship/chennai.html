Chennai Food culture

Chennai's food culture is rich with traditional South Indian cuisine, featuring staples like idli, dosa, and sambar. The city's culinary scene also includes flavorful Chettinad dishes and diverse street food options. Fresh seafood and aromatic spices are integral to its vibrant gastronomy. Additionally, Chennai offers a variety of vegetarian delights, emphasizing the use of coconut, curry leaves, and tamarind. The city's bustling markets and eateries reflect a blend of heritage and modernity in its food traditions.
